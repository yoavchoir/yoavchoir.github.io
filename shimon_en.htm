Mr. Shimon Levtov (Semyon Levyatov) , art director and conductor in 2005-2022, began his musical career at the age of seven as a singer in the well known "Boys' Cappela Choir" in St.Petersburg. He went on to study in the Music Academy of St.Petersburg where he got his Master's Degree in conducting. He later became the conductor of the "Teachers' College for Music" in St.Petersburg and the coordinator of the Conducting Department.

In Israel he conducted the Choir of the Music Academy at Tel Aviv University. Currently he serves as the conductor of "Emek Hefer" choir and of the youth choir at "Alon" High School Music Department. As of 2005 he is the conductor of the  "Yoav" Choir.

Choirs conducted by Mr.Levtov have performed in festivals in Israel and abroad and have participated in prestigious projects together with some of the  leading orchestras in Israel.
