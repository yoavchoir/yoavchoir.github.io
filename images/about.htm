YOAV CHOIR

      • The Yoav choir, founded in 1982 at the initiative of its conductor, Assa barak, represents the Regional Council of Yoav which lies between Jerusalem, Tel Aviv and Beer Sheva.

        The weekly rehearsals bring together 45 members - mostly from the local kibbutzim and Moshavim.

        The choir's repertoire includes a variety of a-cappela works:folk songs and classical pieces from the Renaissance, Baroque and Romantic periods as well as several modern works, along with a substantial selection of Israeli compositions.

        The choir has performed in many locations throughout Israel and has taken part in various choral festivals including the World Assembly of Choirs - the Zimriya (1984,1987, 1988,1992). In the fall of 1989, the choir participated in two international festivals of the Eurotreff: Wolfenbuttel and Stuttgart. In the summer of 1993, the choir took part in the 4th international Europa Cantat in Tabor (Czech Republic), appearing in Prague and its environs. On 1996 the Yoav Choir won a silver diploma in the international competitions Musica Mundi in Riva Del Garda (Italy) and Natanya (Israel).

        Iî August 1998 the choir participated in the Israeli Choir Festival - the ZIMRIYA,and in October 20 1998 - won in a silver diploma in the International Choir competition "Shirat Hayamim" in Natanya (Israel).

        On September 1999 the choir was hosted by Norwegian choirs in a Concert Tour.in Bergen, Hamar and Oslo.
